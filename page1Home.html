Quick Energy Saving Tips

  • Switch to LED lighting
  • Unplug devices when not in use
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Optimize heating and cooling settings
  • Seal drafts around windows and doors

Most Energy-Consuming Devices

  1. Heating and cooling systems
  2. Water heaters
  3. Refrigerators
  4. Washers and dryers
  5. Televisions and entertainment systems

Understanding Energy Ratings

When purchasing new appliances, look for the Energy Rating label. The more stars, the more energy efficient the appliance is compared to other models of similar size.

An appliance with a high star rating may cost more initially but will save you money in the long run through reduced energy bills.
